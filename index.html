Portfolio

EventEaze

EventEaze is an app that find events and the surrounding bars. Events can be saved and then the bars that you want to save to that event will be mapped on Google Maps.

R.Cade

R.cade is an app that helps you search for video games and save it to your profile. Once you are in your profile you can view YouTube videos on your saved games.

Bar Roulette

Bar Roulette looks for a random bar based on search parameters. Then you can get Uber price estimates to the bar while also rendering a Google Map showing starting location and the randomly generated bar.

About Me

Hi I'm Jon, currently a web developer based in New York City. I graduated from General Assembly's Web Development Immersive. Before my transition into web development, I worked in finance, QA testing and IT support. However, when I was at these jobs, I wasn't able to stretch my creativity. Now that I have discovered the world of web development, I have never been happier.

I love to ski. The thrill of going fast and flying off jumps makes me feel alive. On the other end of the spectrum, I also like to play video games. Messing around with friends in a game like GTA is so much fun. Music also plays a big part in my life. Whether I'm walking through the streets of NYC or coding, I always have the music pumping.
